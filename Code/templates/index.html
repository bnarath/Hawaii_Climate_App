Hey There !!! Bincy here ! I am delighted to provide you with the precipitation and temperature data for various stations in Honolulu, Hawaii!!


Check out our APIs:


  • Precipitation data from 2016-08-23 - 2017-08-23; providing the precicitation at various stations and the average.

    /api/v1.0/precipitation

  • List of stations from the dataset.

    /api/v1.0/stations

  • Dates and temperature observations(in Fahrenheit) of the most active station in the last year [2016-08-23 - 2017-08-23].

    /api/v1.0/tobs

  • Minimum temperature, the Average temperature, and the Max temperature starting from the start date [Dates between 2010-01-01 and 2017-08-23]. Modify the start date to the one you want to get data for!

    /api/v1.0/start-date

  • Minimum temperature, the Average temperature, and the Max temperature for a given start to end date range [Dates between 2010-01-01 and 2017-08-23]. Modify the date range to the one you want to get data for!

    /api/v1.0/start-date/end-date
